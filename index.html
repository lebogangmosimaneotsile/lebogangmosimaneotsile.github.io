About

A little about me

My path to UX didn't start with design tools — it started with people. I hold a BA in Psychology and Sociology from the University of Botswana, where I spent four years studying why people think, behave, and interact the way they do.

That curiosity is what brought me to UX. The skills I built — designing research studies, analysing human behaviour, understanding social dynamics — are exactly what good design requires. The best digital products aren't built by people who know the most tools. They're built by people who understand users deeply enough to design for their real needs, not assumed ones.

I bring both the research rigour of a social scientist and the technical ability to bring designs to life in code — which means I can contribute meaningfully at every stage of the product design process.
