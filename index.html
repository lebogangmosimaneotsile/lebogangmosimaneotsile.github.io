About

A little about me

I hold a BA in Psychology and Sociology from the University of Botswana — a background that gives me an edge in UX work. Understanding human behaviour, cognitive patterns and social dynamics isn't just academic for me, it's the foundation of every design decision I make.

I'm currently based in Botswana and building toward a career at the intersection of UX Research, UI Design and Front-End Development, with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction. I believe the best digital products are the ones people don't have to think about.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me following sport, exploring content creation, or deep in a rabbit hole of font pairings on Pinterest.
